How much does social media marketing cost in Ranchi?

In Ranchi, starter social media management retainers typically cost ₹8,000–₹15,000/month, and full-service retainers (content + ads + influencer work + reporting) commonly run ₹25,000–₹1,00,000/month. What should a Ranchi business look for in an agency?

  • A real approval workflow. If approvals happen over WhatsApp screenshots, revisions get lost and posts go out late. The best agencies give you an app where you approve in one tap.
  • Scheduled, automatic posting. Ask whether posts publish automatically at scheduled times or whether someone posts manually — manual posting is how 9 AM posts go out at noon.
  • A monthly report you can read. Reach, engagement and growth in one clean report — generated from real data, not assembled screenshots.
  • Local fluency. Education and retail audiences in Ranchi behave differently — your agency should know that without being told.
  • Clean billing. GST-compliant invoices and digital payment links, not "transfer to this account and WhatsApp the screenshot".
