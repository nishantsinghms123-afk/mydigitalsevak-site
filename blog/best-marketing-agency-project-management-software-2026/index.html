1. My Digital Sevak — best for Indian social media agencies

My Digital Sevak

₹999–4,999/month flat (whole team) · 7-day free trial · built in India

Full disclosure: this is our product. It exists because nothing on this list handles the actual daily loop of a social agency — plan content, get client approval, publish to Instagram, report, invoice with GST. My Digital Sevak does all five in one workspace: Kanban/calendar project tracking, a client mobile app with one-tap approvals and voice-note feedback, Instagram auto-publishing, self-building monthly reports, and Razorpay-powered GST invoicing with milestone payment gates for project work.

Choose it if you're a social-first agency in India. Skip it if you're a 50-person full-service firm needing deep resource utilization reporting — that's Scoro's territory.

2. Scoro — best for large full-service agencies

~$26/user/month · quoting, retainers, utilization

Scoro is the most complete "agency operating system" for firms of 20+ people billing by the hour: quoting, budgets, utilization and financial reporting are genuinely excellent. It assumes a Western workflow — no GST, no UPI, and clients interact by email, not an app.

Choose it for hourly-billing, multi-department agencies with ops staff to run it.

3. Productive — best for tracking agency profitability

~$9/user/month · budgets, margins, resourcing

Productive connects project delivery to margins better than anything near its price — you see which client is profitable in real time. Like Scoro, it stops at delivery: no approvals, no publishing, no client-facing app.

Choose it if per-project profitability is your top question every month.

4. Bonsai — best for freelancers and tiny studios

~$9/user/month · proposals, contracts, invoices

Bonsai wraps proposals, contracts, light project tracking and invoicing into one friendly tool. It's ideal at 1–3 people; agencies outgrow its project depth quickly, and invoicing isn't GST-aware.

Choose it as a solo operator; move on when a team joins.

5–8. ClickUp, Asana, Teamwork, Monday — best for pure task tracking

Free–$11/user/month · tasks, boards, docs

These four are excellent generic project tools with generous free tiers. The catch for agencies: they only see the internal half of the work. Client approvals happen over screenshots, publishing is manual (or another subscription), reporting is another tool again, and none of them invoice. If your agency's work products are social content and campaigns, you'll be paying for 3–4 more tools around them — the math we break down on the agency management software page.

Choose one if you only need task boards and already have systems for approvals, publishing, reporting and billing.

How to choose (60-second version)

  • Social-first agency in India → My Digital Sevak (approvals + publishing + GST billing included)
  • 20+ people, hourly billing, multi-department → Scoro or Productive
  • Solo/freelance → Bonsai
  • Just need task boards → ClickUp or Asana free tier
  • Retainer/client-services with time tracking → Teamwork

Whichever you pick, insist on one thing: the tool should reduce the number of places work lives, not add another tab. An agency's real cost isn't the subscription — it's the handoffs between tools.
