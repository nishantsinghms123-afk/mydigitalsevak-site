About My Digital Sevak:
built by an agency, for agencies.

My Digital Sevak wasn't dreamed up in a boardroom. It was built at 11 PM, between client calls, inside a running Indian marketing agency that was drowning in the same chaos yours probably is.

What is My Digital Sevak?

My Digital Sevak is an all-in-one agency management software made in India by Advance Media Solution. Social media agencies use it to plan and auto-publish content, collect one-tap client approvals through a branded mobile app, track projects and SEO, and bill clients with GST-compliant invoices — all in one workspace, from ₹999/month.

"Sevak" means servant — and that's the whole philosophy. The software serves the agency, handles the repetitive work (posting, reporting, invoicing, chasing approvals), and leaves people free to do the creative work clients actually pay for.

The story

Advance Media Solution is a digital marketing agency that grew to 63+ brands the usual way: WhatsApp approvals, Canva reports assembled at month-end, invoices chased over calls, and a content calendar that lived in three different tools and one person's head.

The tools that promised to fix this were built for Western agencies — priced per seat in dollars, blind to GST and UPI, and with nothing a client could actually use. So the agency built its own system: first the content calendar, then the client approval app, then Instagram auto-publishing, then GST billing with Razorpay, then AI content planning. After running the entire agency on it, that internal tool became My Digital Sevak — agency management software shaped by daily agency life, not by a product roadmap.
