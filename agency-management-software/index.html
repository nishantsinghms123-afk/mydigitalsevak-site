What is agency management software?

Agency management software is a single system that runs a marketing agency's entire client operation — client records, content calendars, project and task tracking, client approvals, reporting and billing. Instead of stitching together spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads and five different subscriptions, everything your team and your clients touch lives in one workspace.

Most agencies discover the problem the same way: work is planned in one tool, approved over WhatsApp screenshots, delivered from Drive links, reported in Canva and billed from a spreadsheet. Every handoff leaks time. Agency management software exists to close those gaps — and the all-in-one dashboard approach means one login replaces the entire stack.
